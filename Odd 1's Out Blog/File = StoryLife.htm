Jame's Life

The Hunters

We were just normal cool dudes at an lazer tag arena.

The thing is, we had to wait for the last session to end.

Meanwhile, there were these fun video game booths!!!

But me and my crew noticed some men in black(Laugh)

So we were starting our game at the lazer tag arena.

We each got to choose our own nicknames, but we never took it seriously.

Do you know what I named myself? A_Girl.

The thing is, when someone shoots you, your gun tells who shot who.

The Hunters sometimes shout: I gonna get ya, girl!

That is how I know who I shot.
